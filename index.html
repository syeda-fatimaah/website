FAQ

Common Questions

What types of projects do you take on?

We work on a wide range of digital projects including custom websites, full-stack web applications, and mobile apps for iOS and Android, as well as social media management campaigns. We specialize in projects that require technical precision and scalable architecture — whether it's a landing page or a complex SaaS platform.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple landing page can be completed in 3–5 business days, while a full-featured web application may take 4–8 weeks. Mobile applications typically range from 6–12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline in every project proposal so you know exactly what to expect before we begin.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. Pioneer Developer operates fully remotely and serves clients from the United States, United Kingdom, UAE, Canada, Australia, Europe, and beyond. All communication is handled professionally via email, video calls, and project management tools. We adapt to your time zone and communication preferences to make collaboration seamless.

Can I hire you directly on Fiverr?

Yes! You can find our services on Fiverr at fiverr.com/faatimaah2004. Fiverr provides a secure payment escrow, clear milestone tracking, and a dispute resolution process — making it a great option for first-time clients. For larger or ongoing projects, direct engagement is also available and often preferred.

What is your development process?

Our process follows four clear phases: Discovery (we understand your goals), Proposal (we define scope, timeline, and price), Build (we develop with weekly progress updates and milestone check-ins), and Launch (delivery, testing, and handoff with post-launch support). We use modern project management tools and maintain full transparency throughout every project.

Do you offer revisions and support after launch?

Yes. Every project includes a defined number of revision rounds as part of the agreed scope. After launch, we provide 30 days of post-launch support to address any bugs or minor adjustments. For ongoing maintenance, we offer monthly retainer packages. We believe in long-term relationships, not one-and-done transactions.
