Process

How Every Project Runs

A consistent, transparent process applied to every engagement — no matter the size or complexity.

1
Step One

Inquiry & Brief

You reach out via our contact form or Fiverr with a description of your project. We respond within 24 hours with clarifying questions to fully understand your goals, timeline, and budget constraints.

2
Step Two

Proposal & Agreement

We send a detailed project proposal outlining scope, deliverables, timeline, payment terms, and total cost. Once you approve, we formalize the agreement and schedule a kick-off call.

3
Step Three

Design Phase

For projects requiring visual work, we start with wireframes and design mockups before writing any code. You review and approve the design direction before we move into development.

4
Step Four

Development Phase

We develop your project in structured milestones, sharing progress updates at each stage. You have access to a staging environment to review live progress at any time during development.

5
Step Five

Review & Testing

Before delivery, we conduct thorough QA testing across devices and browsers. You get a full walkthrough of the completed project and submit any final revision requests within the agreed revision rounds.

6
Step Six

Launch & Handoff

We deploy your project to production, hand over all assets and credentials, and provide 30 days of post-launch support. Long-term maintenance retainer packages are available for ongoing needs.
